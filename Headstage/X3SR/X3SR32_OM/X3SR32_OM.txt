Overview
The X3SR32 is a 32-channel stim-record X-Headstage™ developed by KONTEX, designed for simultaneous neural stimulation and recording in electrophysiological research. Based on two RHS2116 chips from Intan Technologies, the X3SR32 delivers reliable bidirectional data acquisition and output performance in a compact and robust form factor.

Armor
Optional protective covers are available for each headstage, offering extra protection and anchoring. These covers, crafted from Polyoxymethylene (POM), are lightweight and resistant to chemicals. There are three standard armor types, with options for customization in shape and material to suit diverse experimental requirements. It's important to note that the size, weight, and shape of these armors will differ across various X-Headstage™ models.
S Armor
The S armors with ear loops around the side used to anchor headstage to the accessories such as electrode adapters.
M Armor
The M armors are designed to provide protection while maintaining minimal size.
IP Armor
The IP armors are designed to provide the most protection and fully encase the X-Headstage. The X-Headstage™ with the optional parylene coating has been tested to the IP68 standard.
Compatible Acquisition System
The X3SR32 X-Headstage™ is designed for seamless integration with the KONTEX XDAQ™ system, ensuring synchronized stimulation and recording with high fidelity. In addition to the XDAQ™, the X3SR32 is also compatible with a range of open-source and proprietary acquisition systems, provided appropriate signal remapping or interfacing is used. Below is a list of confirmed compatible systems:
Intan Recording Controller (512ch or 1024ch)
Intan Stim-Record Controller
Open-Ephys acquisition box
NeuroNexus Smartbox
Key Features
32 Channels
Enables concurrent recording and stimulation on up to 32 channels.
Dual RHS2116 Architecture
Implements two RHS2116 integrated circuits, each supporting 16 channels, providing flexible and high-fidelity analog front-end performance.
Compact Design
Engineered for minimal weight and size, optimizing for head-mounted or implant-based setups.
Connector
Equipped exclusively with a micro-HDMI connector to interface with the XDAQ™ system.
Protective Cover Option
Can be optionally configured with a protective cover for enhanced durability and anchoring support during in vivo experiments.
