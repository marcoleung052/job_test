§H3§ Overview
The X6R line is KonteX’s high-channel-count X-Headstage™, engineered for advanced neural recording applications requiring 128 channels or more. Despite its high channel density, the X6R maintains a compact form factor, approximately 10 mm x 10 mm and only a few millimeters thick, making it ideal for space-constrained experimental setups.

§H3§ Armor
Optional protective covers are available for each headstage, offering extra protection and anchoring. These covers, crafted from Polyoxymethylene (POM), are lightweight and resistant to chemicals. There are three standard armor types, with options for customization in shape and material to suit diverse experimental requirements. It's important to note that the size, weight, and shape of these armors will differ across various X-Headstage™ models.
§H4§ S Armor
The S armors with ear loops around the side used to anchor headstage to the accessories such as electrode adapters.
§H4§ M Armor
The M armors are designed to provide protection while maintaining minimal size.
§H4§ IP Armor
The IP armors are designed to provide the most protection and fully encase the X-Headstage. The X-Headstage™ with the optional parylene coating has been tested to the IP68 standard.

§H3§ Key Features
§H4§ High Channel Count
Supports 128 channels using two RHD2164 chips, enabling the acquisition of large-scale neural data.
§H4§ Compact Design
Approximately 1 cm square, with minimal thickness to reduce implant burden.
§H4§ Micro-HDMI or Omnetics Connector
Available in both micro-HDMI and optional Omnetics connector configurations to suit various interfacing requirements.
§H4§ 3-Axis Accelerometer
Integrated for real-time activity and motion monitoring.
§H4§ Optional Protective Cover
Can be configured with a protective casing to enhance physical durability and provide additional anchoring points during implantation.
