§H1§ Headstage
The KonteX X-Headstage™ series is a family of high-performance neural recording interfaces designed to provide stable, low-noise signal acquisition across a wide range of experimental configurations.
Engineered for both head-fixed and freely moving animal studies, X-Headstages combine compact mechanical design, flexible connectivity, and scalable channel configurations to support modern electrophysiology research.
The series is fully compatible with the XDAQ acquisition platform and supports integration with multiple probe technologies and adapter ecosystems.
