§H3§ Overview
EIB32-M1S is designed for customers making in-house microdrive hardware and provides electrical connection between the electrode wires and the X-Headstage.
EIB32-M1S Overview Components Dimension: Weight:0.4 g unit: mm
§H3§ Connection Orientation
When plugging the adapter into the EIB, ensure that the side with the X-Headstage is aligned with the silkscreen print mark “G.”
The triangle on the adapter and the EIB should be on the same side, even if the triangle on the EIB is partially covered.
§H3§ Compatible Headstages
This EIB can be connected to X3R32 or X3R64 X-Headstages with the Adpt-X3R32-M1P32. To use with X3SR32 X-Headstage, SR Remapper Adapter must be used in combination.
