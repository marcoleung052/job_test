§H3§ Overview
The EIB SR Right 32ch is a 32-channel Electrode Interface Board (EIB) specifically designed for use with the KonteX X3SR stim-record headstage. This model is configured with the extension on the right side of the headstage, providing flexible layout options in constrained or custom implant environments.
§H3§ EIB32SR Overview
§H3§ Connector
§H4§ The EIB SR Right 32ch is compatible with the following headstages:
X3SR32 Headstage
This EIB is not compatible with XR-series headstages or left-sided SR EIB variants. Always verify the headstage type and channel configuration before installation.
