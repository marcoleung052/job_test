§H3§ Overview
The X3 Headstage Stimulation Test Module is designed to verify the stimulus waveform generated by X3SR headstages. By connecting the module to a dummy 1 kΩ resistive load, users can safely observe and validate stimulation output characteristics without connecting to biological tissue.
Tester-Stim-X3 Overview
§H3§ Test Operation
Due to the keyed connector pin orientation, the X3 Headstage supports left- and right-side connections. Users should alternate the connection orientation to verify stimulation waveforms on each side, ensuring complete testing while preventing incorrect insertion or electrical misalignment.
