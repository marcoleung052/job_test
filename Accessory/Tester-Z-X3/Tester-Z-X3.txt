§H3§ Overview
The X3 Headstage 64-Channel Impedance Test Module is designed to evaluate the impedance measurement functionality of X3 headstages. By connecting the module to dummy test loads with resistive and capacitive (R/C) values ranging from kΩ to MΩ, users can verify impedance measurement accuracy without connecting to electrodes or biological tissue.
Tester-Z-X3 Overview
§H3§ Test Operation
Due to the keyed orientation of the connector pins, impedance testing must be performed in left and right orientations separately. Users should alternate the connection orientation of the X3 Headstage 64-Channel Impedance Test Module to verify impedance measurements on both sides, ensuring complete and accurate validation.
