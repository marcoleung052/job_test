§H1§ Commutator PRO Firmware Update
2026-05-08
§H3§ Overview
This manual provides the step-by-step procedure to update the firmware on your Commutator Pro. Please ensure you have the correct
.uf2
update file provided by KonteX before proceeding.
§H3§ Preparation
Ensure your Commutator Pro is powered off before starting the process.
Prepare a high-quality USB cable to connect the device to your PC.
A paperclip or a similar thin tool is required to access the firmware update button.
Locate the Firmware Update Button (pinhole) on the device interface.
§H3§ Step-by-Step Update Procedure
1. Connect to PC: Plug the USB cable from the Commutator Pro's USB port into your computer.
2. Enter Bootloader Mode:
Use a thin tool (like a paperclip) to press and hold the pinhole button.
While holding the button, switch the Power on.
3. Identify Device: Your PC will recognize the device as a new removable drive named "RPI-RP2". You can now release the pinhole button.
4. Deploy Firmware: Drag and drop (or copy) the provided .uf2 firmware file directly into the "RPI-RP2" drive.
5. Installation: Wait for the file transfer to complete. The drive will automatically disappear from your file explorer once the update is processed.
6. Finalize Update: Toggle the power switch to Restart the Commutator. Your device is now updated and ready for use.
NOTE:
Do not disconnect the USB cable or power off the device while the drive is active and the file is being transferred, as this may result in firmware corruption.
