§H3§ Overview
The NeuroNexus X3 Dual 16ch Probe Adapter is designed to remap a single 32-channel X3 input connector into two independent 16-channel interfaces, enabling the use of two NeuroNexus X-series 16-channel probes with X3 headstages.
This adapter expands probe compatibility while preserving signal integrity, allowing researchers to flexibly integrate multiple low-channel-count probes within an X3-based acquisition system.

Compatibility: X3 Headstage NeuroNexus X-series 16-channel probes Key Features Probe Compatibility Expansion: Allows a single 32-channel X3 input to support two 16-channel NeuroNexus X-series probes.
§H4§ Channel Remapping
Internally remaps channels to ensure correct and deterministic signal routing from each probe to the acquisition system.
Compact and Modular Design: Maintains a small footprint suitable for space-constrained experimental setups. Signal Integrity Preservation: Designed with matched routing to minimize crosstalk and maintain low-noise performance.
