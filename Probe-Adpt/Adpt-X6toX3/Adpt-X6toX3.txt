Adpt-X6toX3
2025-12-29
Overview
The Adapter X6 to Dual X3 is designed to convert the 128-channel input interface of the X6 headstage into two independent 64-channel X3 input ports. This enables full compatibility with existing X3-series accessories, probes, EIBs, and adapter modules.
The adapter preserves signal integrity through matched routing, ensuring that recording performance remains consistent with native X3 headstage configurations.

Allows researchers to use their existing X3 ecosystem with X6-based high-density recording systems.

Differential routing and controlled impedance help maintain low-noise and high-fidelity recording performance.
