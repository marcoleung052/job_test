Probe Adapter
The KonteX Probe Adapter Series provides flexible signal interfacing solutions between neural probes and KonteX X-Headstage™ systems.
Designed to expand probe compatibility across multiple connector formats, channel configurations, and reference architectures, these adapters enable seamless integration of diverse electrode technologies into the KonteX acquisition ecosystem.
Probe Adapters serve as modular interface bridges, ensuring correct signal routing, stable electrical connectivity, and consistent recording performance.
