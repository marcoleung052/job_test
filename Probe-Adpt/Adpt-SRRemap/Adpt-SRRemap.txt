Overview
The SR Headstage Remapper is designed specifically for use with the X3SR32 headstage. It enables compatibility with standard probe adapters that lack the "SR" (Stim-Record) designation. This adapter enables users to connect SR-type headstages to a wider range of compatible adapters and also functions as a headstage cycle extender.

Key Functions
Probe Compatibility Expansion
Allows the X3SR32 headstage to interface with standard (non-SR) probe adapters.
Connector Cycle Extension
Serves as a protective, disposable intermediary that extends the connector life of the X3SR32 headstage during frequent connection and disconnection cycles.
Pin Map
Identified by X3SR to X3R on PCB
