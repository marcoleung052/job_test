§H3§ Overview
This adapter enables X3R32/X3R64-HDMI X-Headstage with KonteX HDMI cables to connect to the Intan Recording Controller directly or via the SPI cable.

§H3§ Usage Scenarios
The RHD connectors are recessed into a slot on the board, enabling direct plug-in to the acquisition box. It can be used to transmit the recording signals from the KonteX's XR headstage to the acquisition system through the HDMI port on the adapter.
