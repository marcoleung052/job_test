§H3§ Overview
This adapter enables XSR32-HDMI X-Headstage with KonteX HDMI cables to connect to the Intan Stimulation/Recording Controller directly or via the SPI cable.
§H3§ SAdpt-HDMI-OM16 Overview

unit: mm
§H3§ Usage Scenarios
The RHS connector is recessed into a slot on the board, enabling direct plug-in the adapter into the acquisition box. It can be used to transmit the recording signals and stimulation functions from the KonteX's XSR headstage to the Intan RHS Stimulation/Recording cpntroller system through the HDMI port on the adapter.
