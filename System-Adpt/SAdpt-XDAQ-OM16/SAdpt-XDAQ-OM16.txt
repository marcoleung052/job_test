§H3§ Overview
The XDAQ™ to Intan RHS Headstage Adapter is designed to interface the XDAQ acquisition system with Intan’s RHS headstages via Stim SPI cables. This adapter provides a direct and reliable connection path, enabling precise stimulation and recording control while maintaining compatibility with Intan’s standard hardware architecture.

§H3§ Usage Scenarios
The SAdpt-XDAQ-OM16 adapter provides a direct and reliable interface between Intan RHS headstages and the XDAQ system. It can be used in experimental setups where stable high- channel-count recording is required, and it also supports extended configurations through optional Stim SPI cables, allowing flexible positioning of the headstages relative to the acquisition system.
